      How it works.

      OTOMO has positioned itself as a one-stop solution for all kinds of four-wheeler needs making it India No1 Car Service Centre.

      Choose Outlet

      Choose your preferred OTOMO outlet from the list on your app.

      Book Appointment

      Select the required service/repairs from the list. Select your preferred time.

      Track Service

      Get real-time updates on your phone. You can track the progress on your app.

      Pay Online

      On service completion, make an online payment through the app.
